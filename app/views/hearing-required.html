Do you want a hearing?

If you go to the hearing you can explain your appeal in person. If you don’t go, the tribunal will make a decision based on the information they already have.

Support can be arranged for you such as an interpreter, hearing loop or an accessible tribunal room. You can bring friends or family to support you.
