The tribunal’s view on your appeal


The tribunal have considered all the information submitted by you and DWP.

Their view is that you’re entitled to PIP at the rate below from 13 February 2018 until 12 February 2020.

Daily living Standard rate
Mobility No award

This is more than DWP initially awarded you.

If both you and DWP accept this view, you’ll be awarded PIP at the above rate.

The reasons for the tribunal’s view

We considered all the evidence you and DWP submitted in relation to your appeal.

We accept that you have more difficulties with daily living because of your shoulder pain. We have therefore increased the points you were awarded for the activity of preparing food.

PIP activities and the descriptors we consider apply to your appeal
Activity Descriptor Points
Preparing food Needs supervision or assitance to either prepare or cook a simple meal 4
Washing and bathing Needs an aid or appliance to wash or bathe 2
Dressing and undressing Needs an aid or appliance for dressing or undressing 2

What happens next

You can accept the view or if you feel the view is wrong you can have a face to face hearing.


Do you accept this view?

You have to respond to this question by 2 May 2018. If you don’t respond we will assume you are accepting the view and it will be sent to you.
