The reasons for our decision

We reached the decision through considering the evidence submitted in relation to your back pain. Specifically the letter your doctor wrote about the painkillers he has prescribed you for your back pain.

While we acknowledge you suffer pain doing some activities, it’s our view that it does not hinder your ability to the degree necessary to change your entitlement to the enhanced rate of daily living.
